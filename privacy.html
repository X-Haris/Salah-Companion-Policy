Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Salah Companion ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of our mobile application, Salah Companion (the "App"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about the data we collect and why.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

User Profile and Account Data (Optional)

The App includes optional social and gamification features, such as a user profile, avatar, and public leaderboards. These features are entirely voluntary. If you choose to create a profile and join the leaderboards, we collect the following information:

  • Display Name: The name you choose to be identified by on the leaderboards. This will be visible to other users. You may also participate with the default name "Anonymous".
  • Account Information: We use Firebase Authentication to securely manage your account via an anonymous sign-in. This creates a unique User ID to associate with your profile data but does not require your email address.
  • User-Generated Content: This includes your avatar configuration, earned points (Hasanat), and unlocked achievements. This data is associated with your profile.

Location Data

To calculate accurate prayer times and determine the Qibla direction, we collect your device's precise location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) and approximate location (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION). While this information is used by the app for its core features, it is also shared with our third-party advertising partner (Google AdMob) to provide location-based ads.

Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we and our third-party service providers automatically collect certain information. This includes:

  • Device or other IDs: Our advertising partner, Google AdMob, collects your device's unique advertising identifier to serve personalized advertisements.
  • App info and performance: We collect crash logs and diagnostics data (e.g., device model, OS version) to diagnose and fix bugs, and to monitor and improve the App's stability and performance.
  • App interactions: We collect anonymized data about how you interact with the App's features to improve user experience. Our advertising partner also collects data on how you interact with ads.
  • Inferred Religious Beliefs: The context of this app (an Islamic utility) may be used by our advertising partner to infer your interests and serve more relevant ads.

User-Generated Content (Local)

The App allows you to create bookmarks. This information is generated by you and is stored only locally on your device. It is never collected by us or shared with any third party unless you voluntarily join the leaderboards.

Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases (such as Premium), Google Play processes and stores your purchase history. We receive confirmation of your purchase status but do not have access to your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide App Functionality: To provide you with accurate, location-based prayer times and Qibla direction.
  • To Provide Social and Gamification Features (Optional): If you choose to join the leaderboards, we use your Display Name, points, country, and avatar to display your rank on public leaderboards and to create a more engaging, personalized experience within the app.
  • For Advertising or Marketing: To display ads within the App, which helps fund development and keep the app free. This includes using your Advertising ID and Location Data to serve personalized ads.
  • For Analytics: To understand how our app is used, diagnose and fix bugs or crashes, and make future performance improvements.
  • For Personalization: To customize your experience, such as showing prayer times based on your location or showing more relevant advertisements.
  • To Provide Premium Features: To verify your purchase status and unlock premium features such as detailed statistics and home screen widgets.

3. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Location ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION
    Required to automatically determine your geographical position for accurate prayer times and Qibla direction.
  • Notifications POST_NOTIFICATIONS
    Necessary to show you timely alerts for daily prayer times and other important Islamic events.
  • Set Exact Alarms SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM
    Used to ensure that prayer time notifications are delivered precisely on time, even when your device is in a low-power mode.
  • Run at Startup RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
    Allows the App to automatically reschedule your prayer alarms after your device is restarted.
  • Internet INTERNET
    Required to fetch prayer time calculation data, download on-demand audio files, display advertisements, and interact with leaderboard and avatar services.
  • Vibrate VIBRATE
    Used to provide vibrating alerts for notifications.
  • Wake Lock & Foreground Service WAKE_LOCK FOREGROUND_SERVICE
    Used to ensure that prayer time alarms and notifications can be reliably delivered even when the app is in the background or the screen is off.
  • In-App Purchases com.android.vending.BILLING
    Required to process premium purchases through Google Play.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with the following third-party service providers:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore): If you opt-in to the leaderboard feature, we use Firebase to create and manage your user account, store your profile data (display name, points, achievements, avatar configuration, country), and power the public leaderboards.
  • DiceBear API: To generate your custom avatar, we send your configuration choices to the DiceBear API. This information is used solely for the purpose of generating the avatar image.
  • Google AdMob: We partner with Google AdMob to serve ads. Google may use the information it collects (such as your Advertising ID and Location) to personalize the ads you see. You can learn more about how Google collects and uses data by visiting their policy page: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.
  • Google Play Services: We use services provided by Google Play to analyze crash logs and app performance data to improve the app's stability.
  • Google Play Billing: We use Google Play's billing system to process in-app purchases. Google handles all payment processing. We only receive confirmation of purchase status.

5. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete all your data directly within the app:

Settings > Delete My Data

This action will permanently delete:

  • All locally stored data: Prayer history, achievements, bookmarks, reading progress, avatar customizations, and settings
  • Leaderboard profile (if joined): Your leaderboard profile, display name release, and Firebase sign-out

Alternative methods:

  • Clear the app's data in your device's system settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

For questions about data deletion or to request confirmation of permanent deletion from our servers, please contact us at:

support@abduloski.dev
