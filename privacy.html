Privacy Policy for Salah Companion

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Salah Companion ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of our mobile application, Salah Companion (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Automatically Collected Information: When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information about your device and your usage of the App through third-party services. This includes:

    • Crash Information (via Firebase Crashlytics): We collect information related to app crashes and performance, such as device state information, unique device identifiers, and location of the crash. This helps us diagnose and fix bugs to improve the App's stability.
    • Usage Data (via Firebase Analytics): We collect anonymized data about how you interact with the App, such as the features you use and the time, frequency, and duration of your activities. This helps us understand what features are most popular and how to improve the user experience.
    • Device Information: This includes your device model, operating system version, and other technical information.

  • Information You Provide to Us: Currently, the App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information like your name or email address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve the App: We use crash reports and analytics data to maintain the App, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • To Understand Our Users: Analytics data helps us understand our user base and make informed decisions about the future direction of the App.

3. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Internet Access (`android.permission.INTERNET`): This permission is required to download on-demand audio files for recitations that are not bundled with the initial app installation.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share the automatically collected, anonymized information with the following third-party service providers:

  • Google Firebase: We use Firebase services (Crashlytics and Analytics) to help us analyze and improve the App. Their use of information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. While no system is completely secure, we take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
