Privacy Policy for Salah Companion

Effective Date: August 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Salah Companion ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of our mobile application, Salah Companion (the "App"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about the data we collect and why.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: We collect your device's precise location (`ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION`) and approximate location (`ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION`) to calculate accurate prayer times and determine the Qibla direction. This information is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.
  • Automatically Collected Information: When you use the App, we and our third-party service providers automatically collect certain information about your device and your usage of the App. This includes:
    • Advertising Data (via Google AdMob): Our advertising partner, Google AdMob, collects device information, such as your IP address and a unique advertising identifier, to serve advertisements. This helps support the App and keep it free.
    • Crash Information (via Firebase Crashlytics): We collect information related to app crashes and performance, such as device state information, unique device identifiers, and location of the crash. This helps us diagnose and fix bugs to improve the App's stability.
    • Usage Data (via Firebase Analytics): We collect anonymized data about how you interact with the App, such as the features you use and the time, frequency, and duration of your activities. This helps us understand what features are most popular and how to improve the user experience.
    • Device Information: This includes your device model, operating system version, and other technical information.
  • Information You Provide to Us: The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information like your name or email address. Any data you enter, such as fast tracking or favorites, is stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Core App Functionality: We use your location data to provide you with accurate, location-based prayer times and Qibla direction.
  • To Send Timely Reminders: We use notification and alarm permissions to deliver reliable alerts for prayer times and other important Islamic events.
  • To Provide and Improve the App: We use crash reports and analytics data to maintain the App, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • To Display Advertisements: We use Google AdMob to display ads within the App, which helps fund the development and maintenance of the App.
  • To Understand Our Users: Analytics data helps us understand our user base and make informed decisions about the future direction of the App.

3. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Location (`ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` and `ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION`): Required to automatically determine your geographical position. This allows the App to provide accurate prayer times and the Qibla direction for your specific location.
  • Notifications (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`): Necessary to show you timely alerts for daily prayer times, fasting reminders (Suhoor/Iftar), and other important Islamic events.
  • Set Exact Alarms (`SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`): Used to ensure that prayer time notifications are delivered precisely on time, even when your device is in a low-power or doze mode. This is crucial for the reliability of prayer alerts.
  • Run at Startup (`RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED`): Allows the App to automatically reschedule your prayer alarms after your device is restarted. This ensures you do not miss any prayer notifications if your phone turns off and on again.
  • Internet (`INTERNET`): Required to fetch prayer time calculation data, download on-demand audio files, and display advertisements to support the app.
  • Bluetooth (`BLUETOOTH_CONNECT`): This permission may be used to enhance your experience by connecting to Bluetooth-enabled devices, such as headphones for listening to Quran recitations.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data like precise location. We may share automatically collected, anonymized information with the following third-party service providers:

  • Google AdMob: We partner with Google AdMob to serve ads. Google may use the information it collects to personalize the ads you see. You can learn more about how Google collects and uses data by visiting their policy page: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.
  • Google Firebase: We use Firebase services (Crashlytics and Analytics) to help us analyze and improve the App. Their use of information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. All sensitive data, such as your location, is processed on your device and not transmitted to our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
